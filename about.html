About Me

My name is Cooper Miller. I am a student at SCC and am majoring in Computer Information Technology (CIT). I'm originally from Axtell, NE, small town of about 750 people. I grew up being very active with sports and extracurricular activities. Some of my hobbies though included playing video games. I first got into technology at age 14 when me and my brother built PCs from scratch. Although I basically just followed exactly what he did, it was an eye opener to me. I later tried to create my very own video game using the Unity Engine. Even though no project came out of this, it was still a very interesting thing to me. When choosing a major/career to get into, I looked back to when I was a kid. I realized maybe CIT was a good place to go in life, and after going to SCC, I realized I really enjoy creating something from the ground up. I'm very passionate about software development and creating an application from nothing. From basic html/css websites, to game development, it all interests me and I strive to learn it all.
